Activists





Zhang Huimei

Zhang Huimei, better known by her nickname A-mei. She is a Taiwanese Puyuma singer and songwriter. She uses her popularity to help the LGBT community by organising a fundarising campaign.

Chi Chia Wei

Chi Chia Wei is a Taiwanese civil rights activist. In 1986, he became the first person in Taiwan to come out as gay on national TV,organising a press conference to announce his sexuality and launch of a campaign to prevent the spread of AIDS/HIV.

Cai Yilin

Cai Yilin is a Taiwanese singer,songwriter, dancer, actress as well as businesswoman. She has a dedicated fanbase composed mostly by female and gay people. In 2007, Tasi's "Bravo Lover" became the theme song of the 2007 Taiwan Pride parade. Additionally, some of her songs cater to the gay audiance and support same-sex love.

Ho Chuen-juei

Ho Chuen-juei and her nickmane is Josephine Ho. She is the chair of the English department of National Central University,Taiwan, and the coordinartor of its Center For the Study of Sexualities. She is one of the most known feminist scholrs in Taiwan and also called "the godmother of the Taiwanese queer movement."

Yu Mei-nu

Yu Mei-nu is a Taiwanses politician. She is a staunch supporter for the legalisation of same-sex marriage in Tawian, and known for her advocacy of women's rights.

Zhu Huiliang

Zhu Huiliang and her nickname is Josephine Chu. She is a Taiwanese former politician. She has worked to expand LGBT rights in Taiwan as well as has devocated for rights of foreign spouses.
