LGBT+ in Taiwan

About Us

This website is to keep you informed about current legislative changes taking place in Taiwan, concerning LGBT+ rights. This website contains updates and information on the current referendum in Taiwan on same-sex marriage which is due to take place on 23 November 2018, and this website will comprise the following to help keep on top of developments:

  • Give a brief background on the referendum.
  • Show who the key supporters of LGBT+ rights are in Taiwan.
  • Give links to live media updates and social media relevant for LGBT+ rights in Taiwan.
