About Us

This website is to keep you informed about current legislative changes taking place in Taiwan, concerning LGBT+ rights. More specifically, this website contains updates and information on the current situation in Taiwan on same-sex marriage, and will comprise the following to help keep on top of developments:

  • A brief background on the referendum; consequences and action.
  • The key supporters of LGBT+ rights in Taiwan.
  • Links to live media updates and social media platforms relevant for LGBT+ rights in Taiwan.
