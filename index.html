This website is to keep you informed about current legislative changes taking place in Taiwan, concerning LGBT+ rights. This website contains updates and information on the current referendum in Taiwan on same-sex marriage which is due to take place on 23 November 2018, and this website will comprise the following to help keep on top of developments:

  • Give a brief background on the referendum.
  • Show who the key supporters of LGBT+ rights are in Taiwan.
  • Give live updates on social media relevant to LGBT+ rights in Taiwan.
  • Give a little brief background into who we are.

Rainbow Flag meaning

The rainbow flad was popularised as a symble of the the gay community by San Francisco in 1978. The different colours are often associated with "diversity" in the gay community. The colours were determined to symbolise:

  • Red means life
  • Orange means healing
  • Yellow means sunlight
  • Green means nature
  • Blue means harmony/ peace
  • Purple means spirit
