Rainbow flag meaning

The rainbow flag was popularised as a symbol of the gay community in San Francisco in 1978. The different colours are associated with diversity in queer communities. The colours are determined to symbolise the following:

  • Red means life
  • Orange means healing
  • Yellow means sunlight
  • Green means nature
  • Blue means harmony/peace
  • Purple means spirit

Pink, white, brown and black have also been added to pride flags recently, with pink representing sex; white representing peace; and brown and black representing people of colour.
